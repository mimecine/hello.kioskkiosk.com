KIOSK HELLO

Holiday 2011

Written on December 6, 2011 by mimecine

Oboy, it’s time for this again:

KIOSK
No kidding, it’s the holidays again, have you noticed? Are you taking the time to enjoy them or are you simply
ignoring Santa? This year we want you to enjoy the holidays. The greater good needs some happiness. Think of all of us when you make cookies, eat with friends, drink glögg and wrap gifts. Here at Kiosk we have been speeding it up. The place is decorated, we have many new things for the season, Chris is hanging the wreath, Nate is starting on the gift bags, Marco has gone digital and Alisa is passed out in the hammock chair. The Polkagris
came over with a friend, Noa’s calendar is back, there is something called Tears of Love, well, you
can see it all HERE. We can also offer: fun, odd things, conversation, holiday advice,
gift advice, sweets, toys and joys. If you need any of the above stop in, write us or call, seriously, we enjoy that stuff. Contact info below.

This is a coupon for 15% off at KIOSK to say thank you.

Bring it in when you come by or type in “Hohoho” when online.

Use the savings to treat someone to something, pass it on.

Yuki has done it again with “Yuki’s Amazing Christmas Shop”. Vintage items. This year she worked with O.C. and David from the 25th Street Flea Market. What they have come up with is nothing short of an extravaganza. You can see it HERE. If you are not in the mood for Christmas now you will be soon…
You can find us at 95 Spring Street every day until the 25th of December from 11 – 8pm. Yep, that’s right, we are open on Sundays and an extra hour every day too. My goodness. Happy Holidays!

KIOSK, HOLIDAY HOURS 11-8PM ALL DAYS. LOCATED AT 95 SPRING STREET ON THE 2ND FLOOR BETWEEN MERCER AND BROADWAY. PHONE: 212.226.8601. EMAIL: INFO@KIOSKKIOSK.COM.
